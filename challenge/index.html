Little challenges to test your programming skills

The Programming Challenges are programs that anybody can make, but they have to follow the guidelines listed below. The purpose of the challenges is to test your programming skills in BASIC and to see who can make the best program with the given criteria. The challenges will begin at the beginning of every month a will last only three weeks. After all of the programs have been submitted, the winner will be announced within a few days, with his/her program posted on this page. All the entered programs will have the option of becoming a true program and being placed in the Program Archives.

Rules:

  • You MAY NOT ask anybody for help with the program because the whole purpose is to see how well YOU can program, not you and your helper.
  • The program must include some sort of title
  • It must follow allow of the guidelines or it will not be considered
  • Unless a specific size maximum is stated, the size of the program does not matter. However, the more optimal the code, the better.
  • The program must be sent zipped accompanied by a text file that has your name and email address to challenge@basicguruonline.com by the 21st of the month or it will not be considered
