Warriors v1.0
Created by Michael J.Ford

Discription:

Warriors is one of my best games. First, you have to buy a sword, shield,
and gauntlets. Then you are off on your misson!

Controls:

When moving:
left-left
right-right
up-up
down-down
mode-exit

When shopping:
ENTER-buy
Mode-leave shop
Del-exit

When fighting:
left-use sword
right-block
Del-leave the fight
Mode-exit
:End
