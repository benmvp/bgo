What do I do if the FAQ does not solve my problem?
If none of the FAQs answer your question, which could be possible, especially if you're asking a question about a program you're trying to make, then you should just use the Questions form to send me your question. If you question, however, would be a question that other users my have, then I'll most likely add it to the FAQ section.
