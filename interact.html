Welcome to the BASIC Guru Online Visitor and Member Interaction page. In this page are links to five different types of inteaction. You can sign and view the BASIC Guru Online guestbook, you can ask some questions in the BASIC Guru Online message board, and you can have a talk with other visitors of BASIC Guru Online in the chat room. You can also participate in the Programming Challenge or post Unofficial News about your site.

Programming Challenge
Participate in the Programming Challenges in order to test your skills at programming in BASIC.
General Message Board
Do you have a question that you feel that a lot of others might have also? You can post your question in the BASIC Guru Online General Message Board. Questions posted on the General Message Board usually are replied to within 6-12 hours depending on when you post it.
Program Idea Message Board
Do you need some ideas for a program that you want to create? Do you have some ideas for a program, but just don't know how to create it? Either way, you can post a request for ideas for a program you're making or post some ideas that you have for a program.
Unofficial News
News posted by visitors of BASIC Guru Online about things they feel are important to them. Anybody can post news about their site, a program they have released, or anything else that you feel is important, as long as it is TI-calculator related.
Chat Room
Just want to talk to other visitors of this site about TI-related things? Well, this is the place to visit. Here in the BASIC Guru Online chat room, you can asks questions and get immediate responses or just talk about anything you feel like. The chat room is open 24 hours a day, but there is a certain time where you'll be able to ask me questions. If you see the UserName "Benahimvp" in the Chat Room, that means that I am logged into it. If you talk and I do not respond quickly just wait. I check to see if there's anyone in the Chat Room periodically.
Weekdays: 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM Central Time
Weekends: Noon - 10:00 PM Central Time

Note: I may not be in the Chat Room during the whole time period.
